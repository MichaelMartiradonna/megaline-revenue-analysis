# Megaline Prepaid Plan Revenue Analysis

**Project Goal**  
Analyzed client behavior in 2018 to determine which of Megaline’s two prepaid plans (Surf or Ultimate) brings in more revenue.

**Key Technologies**  
- Python, Pandas, NumPy  
- Matplotlib & Seaborn  
- SciPy (statistical hypothesis testing)

**Key Findings**  
- Surf users regularly exceed their data limit and generate large overage fees.  
- Despite the lower base price, **Surf produces significantly higher average monthly revenue** per user.  
- Both statistical hypotheses confirmed: revenue differs between plans and between regions.

**Business Recommendation**  
Allocate the larger share of the advertising budget to the **Surf** plan — it is clearly the more profitable plan for Megaline.

**How to Run**  
Open the Jupyter notebook in Google Colab or Jupyter Notebook.
